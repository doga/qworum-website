Qworum Privacy Policy

Definitions

  • Qworum (hereinafter the "Platform") is a microservices platform.
  • Armangil Software (hereinafter "Company") is the software company that provides the Plaform as platform-as-a-service.
  • The owner of a Qworum-enabled website is hereinafter designated as the "Customer".
  • Qworum Business (hereinafter the "Subscription") is a subscription plan that the Customer can purchase from the Company for enabling the Platform on internet and intranet websites.

Collected data

The mandatory data that the Company collects from the Customer include:

  • The Customer name,
  • The Customer address,
  • The Customer's email address,
  • The DNS domain(s) that is/are the object(s) of the Subscription(s).

The optional data that the Company collects from the Customer include:

  • The Customer's bank account identifier,
  • The Customer's bank account holder name,
  • The Customer's bank account holder address.

Use of collected data

The Company uses the collected mandatory data for managing Subscriptions.

The Company uses the collected optional data for reimbursements.

Transmission of collected data to third parties

The Company does not transmit any Customer data to any third parties, except for the payment services providers whose services the Company uses for managing Subscriptions.
